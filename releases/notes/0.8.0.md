## 亮点
- `0.8.0` 把 Island 从提醒面板推进到控制面：Notch 文件投递、审批 review、Hook Health Center、精确跳转、移动中继和硬件模拟器都进入同一个本地工作流。
- 新增 Smart Suppress、安静时段与跟随焦点策略，自动展开、声音和提醒现在通过统一的 attention policy 判断。
- 文件或图片可以直接拖到 Island，再选择活跃 session 投递本地路径。

## 集成
- Hook Health Center 会显示 installed、missing、stale、conflict 状态，并支持重装、卸载与 JSON 诊断导出。
- 新增 Kimi、Factory、Trae、StepFun、AntiGravity hook profile。
- 精确跳转扩展到 Warp、WezTerm/Kaku、Zellij 和 JetBrains workspace。

## 实验能力
- 新增自托管 relay client、Node relay 服务和 iOS/watch companion skeleton。
- 新增硬件状态协议和 macOS 模拟窗口，本轮不包含真实 ESP32 固件。

## Highlights
- `0.8.0` turns the Island into more of a control surface with notch file drop, approval review, Hook Health Center, precision jumps, mobile relay, and a hardware simulator.
- Smart Suppress, Quiet Hours, and Follow Focus now share one attention policy for auto-open, notification, and sound decisions.
- Files and images can be dropped onto the Island and routed into an active session as local paths.

## Integrations
- Hook Health Center reports installed, missing, stale, and conflict states, with reinstall, uninstall, and JSON diagnostics export.
- Adds Kimi, Factory, Trae, StepFun, and AntiGravity hook profiles.
- Precision jumps now cover Warp, WezTerm/Kaku, Zellij, and JetBrains workspace routing.

## Experimental
- Adds a self-host relay client, a minimal Node relay service, and iOS/watch companion skeletons.
- Adds a hardware status protocol and macOS simulator window. Real ESP32 firmware is intentionally out of scope for this release.
