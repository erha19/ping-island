## 更新
- 匿名统计改为每日设备级快照，每台设备每天最多上传一次。
- 每日快照只保留活跃设备、客户端/Provider 会话数、tmux 会话数、离岛宠物/刘海屏模式以及设置开关调整次数。

## 说明
- 关闭匿名统计会清除本地尚未上传的每日聚合数据。
- 不再上传打开/关闭 Island、审批、安装 hook 或单次会话完成等细粒度事件。

## Updates
- Anonymous telemetry now uploads daily device-level snapshots, at most once per device per day.
- Daily snapshots only keep active-device, client/provider session counts, tmux session counts, surface mode, and setting-toggle adjustment counts.

## Notes
- Turning anonymous telemetry off clears pending local daily aggregates.
- Island open/close, approval, hook install, and per-session completion events are no longer uploaded separately.
